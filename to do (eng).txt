1. LOADING
	- LOGO
	- COLORS
	- FONT
	
2. LOCATION OF THE ESTABLISHMENT
	- FIXED BAR WITH ESTABLISHMENT LOGO AND NAME
	- FIELD FOR TABLE NUMBER
	- FIELD FOR NUMBER OF PEOPLE
	- FIELD WITH FORM TO ENTER NAMES OF PEOPLE AT THE TABLE
	- ENTER BUTTON
	- SOCIAL MEDIA BUTTONS
	
3. MENU
	- FIXED BAR WITH OPTION TO GO BACK TO THE PREVIOUS PAGE
	- PAGE TITLE IN BOLD AT THE BEGINNING
	- TABS FOR 'DISHES', 'DRINKS' AND 'DESSERTS'
	- EACH TAB WITH AT LEAST ONE ITEM
	- EACH ITEM WITH PRICE VARIATION
	- EACH ITEM WITH DESCRIPTION
	- EACH ITEM WITH A QUANTITY SELECTOR
	- FOOTER WITH FINAL VALUE OF THE CHOSEN ITEMS
	- BUTTON TO GO TO THE CART LIST
	- BUTTON FOR DIRECT PAYMENT
	
4. CART
	- FIXED BAR WITH HIGHLIGHTED TITLE
	- LIST OF ITEMS GROUPED BY CATEGORY
	- UNIT VALUE OF THE ITEM
	- TOTAL VALUE OF ITEMS WITH MORE THAN ONE SELECTION
	- GENERAL ITEM CHANGE BUTTON
	- ORDER CLOSE BUTTON AND REDIRECT TO PAYMENT PAGE
	
5. PAYMENT
	- FIXED BAR HIGHLIGHTING THE PAGE TITLE
	- TABS WITH PAYMENT OPTIONS
	- CASH:
		FIELD TO ENTER THE AMOUNT TO BE PAID AND IF NECESSARY, THE CHANGE
		PAYMENT CONFIRMATION BUTTON
	- PIX:
		PIX KEY DISPLAY ZONE OF THE ESTABLISHMENT
	- CARD:
		PAYMENT ZONE ACCORDING TO THE REQUIRED API OF THE ESTABLISHMENT
